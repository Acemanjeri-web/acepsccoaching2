Graphic Design Institute in Kerala, Oxdu Integrated Media School

If you're looking to getting into or going for a career jump into designing, then our graphic design institute in kerala are there to train you with all the skills and creativity needed to stand out. Recognizing that a stronger foundation in both conventional methods of design and their advanced AI-backed versions is the key to success at Oxdu Integrated Media School, we focus on that very approach. This will ensure you become good at both, but it will also provide you with tools to stand out in a competitive marketplace.

What You Will Learn in Our Graphic Design Courses


Our comprehensive graphic design courses in Malappuram cover everything you need to know, from design principles to advanced software skills. Whether you are an absolute beginner in graphic design or looking to improve existing expertise, our syllabus will take you step by step:

Introduction to Graphic Design

You'll start with the fundamentals of design theory-donation, typography, and color theory. This is such a foundation to your journey as a designer that even the beginner won't find it difficult.

Adobe Photoshop

Master software is the industry standard for image editing and manipulation. There, you will explore all the ways of creating images, retouch images, or even something as complex as composition.

Adobe Illustrator

Learn vector graphics and logo designing with Adobe Illustrator. This course will teach you how to make scalable artwork for producing anything in web graphics to branding materials.

Adobe InDesign

Learn to lay out and design print-ready materials like brochures, magazines, and eBooks. This course ensures you learn the essentials of print design and desktop publishing.

Web Design Principles

Explore the foundation of web design, including UI/UX essentials, responsiveness, and visual hierarchy. At the end of this course, you'll have learned how to start designing fantastic websites that are functional.

Branding and Identity Design

Discover why brand identity is important and how you can create a powerful business brand. You will polish its message by creating logos, style guides, and marketing materials to better resonate with the target market.

Learn how AI is revolutionizing graphic design and how to merge AI tools into your design workflow seamlessly- from automating tasks to creating unique designs.

Become a freelancer

At last, learn the business of design, from marketing yourself, managing clients, and growing a successful freelancing career in the competitive design industry.

Why Choose Our Graphic Design Institute in Kerala?


Thus, at Oxdu Integrated Media School, we believe to be a community of creatives in our graphic design institute in kerala. The core learning experience here is hands-on learning-from design skills to technical skills-and we ensure you will walk out, confident of tackling projects in the real world. Moreover, AI integrated here, you'd be more advanced in mastering the most potent cutting-edge technologies that form shape design in the future.
